Privacy Policy for Break Reminder

Last updated: [05-Aug-2024]

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Privacy Policy page for the "Break Reminder" Chrome extension. This policy explains how we handle user information and data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe experience while using our extension.

2. No Data Collection

"Break Reminder" does not collect, store, or share any personal information or user data. The extension operates entirely locally on your device and does not transmit any data to external servers or third parties. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personally identifiable information (such as name, email address, phone number)
  • Health information
  • Financial or payment information
  • Location data
  • Web history
  • User activity data
  • Authentication information
  • Personal communications

3. Permissions

The extension requests certain permissions solely to provide its intended functionality. These permissions are not used to collect or transmit any data:

  • notifications: To display break reminders to the user.
  • alarms: To schedule notifications at specific intervals.

4. Use of Permissions

The permissions requested by "Break Reminder" are strictly used for the core functionality of the extension. For instance, the notification permission is used to send reminders for taking breaks, and the alarms permission is used to set timers for these notifications.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
